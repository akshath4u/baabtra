baabtra.com Profile - a measure of your technical expertise

baabtra.com Profile (BP) is a technical diagnostics report that measure technical expertise of any prospective candidate with industry standards. The report will expose candidate's depth of knowledge in any technology that he/she opts to work in the industry.

 

Benefits of BP

  • Free Placement assistance
  • Free career guidance
  • Free interview preparation kit
